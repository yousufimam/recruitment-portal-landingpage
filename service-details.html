Centralized Access for All Stakeholders

AutoRecruit, an AI-based recruitment solution, offers a centralized access system for all stakeholders involved in the recruitment process. This means recruiters, hiring managers, and candidates can access the portal from one platform. This feature streamlines the recruitment process, saving valuable time and effort for everyone involved.

Intuitive User Interface to Search Relevant Vacancies

AutoRecruit's user interface is designed to be intuitive and user-friendly, making it easy for job seekers to search and apply for relevant vacancies. The portal features a powerful search engine that filters jobs based on keywords, location, industry, and other criteria, making it easier for candidates to find the right job.

Comprehensive Candidate Profiling and Resume Building

AutoRecruit's comprehensive candidate profiling and resume-building feature allows candidates to create a detailed profile that showcases their skills, experience, and qualifications. The recruitment portal provides automated assessments to ensure the candidate's skills match the job requirements. This feature also helps employers to identify the most qualified candidates.

Tightly Integrated with all the HRMs

AutoRecruit seamlessly integrates with all kinds of HR systems, providing easy HR management. This feature allows recruiters to track and manage candidate applications and interview schedules from one platform, reducing the risk of errors and saving time.

Flexible Licensing Options

AutoRecruit offers flexible licensing options, making it easier for businesses of all sizes to access our platform. Whether you're a startup, a small business, or a large enterprise, our licensing options are designed to meet your needs and budget.

Chatbot Functionality

What differentiates AutoRecruit from other HR software is its built-in chatbot that can interact with candidates and answer their questions, making the recruitment process more interactive and engaging. It has the ability to respond to all the common queries regarding job posts.

Open API Integration

AutoRecruit offers open API integration, which means it can be integrated with other HR software and applications to automate recruitment tasks, reduce manual efforts and streamline workflows.

Resume Parsing

AutoRecruit can automatically parse resumes and extract key information such as contact details, work experience, education, and skills. This feature saves recruiters a lot of time and effort, allowing them to focus on more important tasks such as interviewing candidates and making hiring decisions
